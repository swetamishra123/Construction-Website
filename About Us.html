About Us

Single Point of Responsibility

We provide design and construction services with single-point responsibility. Many people encounter conflict in the process of achieving their development goals, often putting their project feasibility at risk. We provide design and construction services with single-point control over project responsibilities.
When you maintain control, your schedule, quality, and budget remain in balance, and you reach a successful outcome We know you want your construction project to be successful. In order to do that, you need to minimize your project’s design, source, and build risk factors. In seeking this balance, you may be encountering conflict, putting your project’s feasibility at risk. At SMC, we understand your frustration. You shouldn’t have to choose between meeting your quality, budget and schedule goals, nor should you need to repeat the process multiple times. We understand the complexities involved in project development, that's why we take single-point responsibility over your project’s best value, highest quality, and optimal schedule delivery. The process is simple. Meet with us to discuss your project, get the viability reporting you need to make informed decisions, then, align your project components to reach a successful outcome. Whether the design-build platform is right for you or you are in search of a dependable and trustworthy Construction Manager, SMC has the tools to be your strategic partner. We're going to value engineer your project for the best value, highest quality, and optimal schedule delivery — which means you're going to have what it takes to get this done.
