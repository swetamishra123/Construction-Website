Design Build

SMC partners with your team from program development to concept design through the finished product, and represents you as the single point of responsibility. Whether your project is a new development or renovation of an existing space, you can rely on SMC Construction to help you achieve your goals. One of the major benefits of the design-build delivery method is the ability to work alongside the designers to value engineer your project to meet your budget, while still maintaining objectives. This delivery method incorporates close collaboration between construction and design staff, and reduces time spent on requests for information, submittals, plan revisions, and field changes.
